About Us

At torch we aim to supply new silversmiths with the tools, resources and education to start this skill. We teach new silversmiths the attention to detail, design from start to finish and creating a durable unique piece of wearable art. Founded by Tara Brooks, a silversmith located in Portland Oregon, whose passionate about spreading her craft skills.

Hangout at Torch

Were a big fun group of crafty nerds, come hangout and we will teach you how to get your hands dirty and sometimes bandaged with learning the craft of silversmithing. Below are our services

Workshops

Our workshops range from beginner to expert, we aim to teach all levels of silversmithing with workshops focused on all topics such as stone setting, creating cabochons, and design!

Resources

When joining our crew you will have access to tools, materials and education that can get you started to becoming a silversmith.

Join Us

We are a small but full of creativity community, join us and have access to our studio space and hang out with a small group of makers!

Studio Time

If you become a member at Torch you can earn extra time in our studio by mentoring a new silversmith, or volunteering to keep the studio clean and tidy.

Meet our Makers

Our group of makers range from just starting out silversmithing to years of experience, we make all kinds of pieces from statements to subtle details. Each of our designers engage in our community and are excited to teach others their craft.
